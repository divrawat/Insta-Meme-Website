MeMe Chat App

All of us have seen or heard about Memes Now a days on Instagram , Twitter , facebook , Reddit etc. A MeMe is a Humourous version of a picture , text or a video Edited in a way to make people laugh . Started by 22 year olds Taaran Chanana and Kyle Fernandes MemeChat enables users to make their original memes and also get paid for it . Started In 2019 this App is one of the winners of AatmaNirbhar Bharat App Innovation Challenge.

This App has been downloaded by more than 65 lakh people and have 1,30,000 active users available both on IOS and Android . Meme Chat has a total of 11 full time Employees and 15 part time Employees . The users can make memes using templates available in Meme Chat itself, and get paid upon their approval. As another option, users can earn creating memes in the requested category and get paid by brands on their approvals .

This App also gives a Meme Chat keyboard ( Available in both IOS and Android ) in which users can reply with memes in whats App , Instagram etc .
